Research

Bachelor Thesis · 2025 – present

Large-Scale Structure in the Vela Region

My thesis investigates the large-scale structure in the Vela region using Gaia DR3 data. I apply the SigMA clustering algorithm, developed by Sebastian Ratzenboeck, to identify and characterise stellar groups. The project is implemented in Python and maintained openly on GitHub.

Supervised by Alena Rottensteiner (PhD Student, University of Vienna) · Alves Lab
In preparation

Noise Removal Strategy for SigMA

Upcoming work on developing a noise-removal strategy for the SigMA clustering algorithm, improving its robustness against field contamination in dense stellar environments.
