Research

Large-Scale Structure in the Vela Region

2025 – 2026

Bachelor thesis · Alves Lab, University of Vienna

My thesis investigates the large-scale structure in the Vela region using Gaia DR3 data. I applied the SigMA clustering algorithm, developed by Sebastian Ratzenböck, to identify and characterise stellar groups. Supervised by Alena Rottensteiner (PhD student, University of Vienna).

Noise Removal Strategy for SigMA

ongoing

Alves Lab, University of Vienna

I am developing a noise-removal strategy for the SigMA clustering algorithm, improving its robustness against field contamination in dense stellar environments.
